Frequently Asked Questions

How do token limits work?

Tokens are counted on input + output for each LLM request. Local Ollama models are unlimited. Cloud fallbacks deduct from your quota. Rolls over to next month.

What cloud fallbacks are included?

Growth plan includes Groq, Anthropic, Google Vertex, and OpenAI. Developer includes Groq only. Enterprise gets all plus custom integrations.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade from your dashboard. Changes take effect on the next billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds?

We don't refund partial months. If you're unsatisfied, contact support within 14 days of your first payment.

What Payment methods do you accept?

India: Razorpay (UPI, cards, net banking). International: Stripe (cards, PayPal).

What about Private Node?

Dedicated VPS deployment. Customer is Data Fiduciary. All data stays within your infrastructure. DPA available on request. Contact sales for pricing.
